Who We Are

SRI HARI ENGINEERING CO. was established in 1984 by Mr. P.B. Palekar, to meet the industrial packaging needs in rigid plastics using the thermoforming process. To serve its increased customer base and also to meet higher quality standards, Mr. Palekar along with a few others, co-promoted in 2002, a new company,

SRI HARI PACKAGING INDUSTRIES PVT.LTD. The company has all the required legal registrations under the local laws.

The factory is located at a very modern industrial area developed by the state government about 50km from Pune. Its product range includes packaging and material handling solutions. The company also provides solutions in development and supply of engineering plastic components.
